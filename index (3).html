✦ Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How many revisions are included in each package? +
Each design package includes up to 2 rounds of revisions. Additional revisions are available at KShs 500 per round. I want to ensure you're 100% satisfied with the final product, so we'll work together until it's perfect.
What file formats do you provide? +
All designs are delivered in multiple formats: High-resolution PNG, JPG, PDF, and editable source files (PSD/AI). This ensures your designs are ready for print, web, social media, and any other platform you need.
What's your typical turnaround time? +
Standard turnaround is 2–5 business days depending on project complexity. Rush projects (24–48 hours) are available at a 30% premium. I'll confirm the exact timeline when you submit your brief.
Do you offer unlimited revisions? +
Each package includes 2 revision rounds. After that, additional revisions are KShs 500 each. This ensures quality and allows me to take on more clients while maintaining excellence.
Can you work with tight deadlines? +
Absolutely! I offer rush services for urgent projects. 24-hour turnaround is available at a 30% premium, and 48-hour at 20% premium. Just mention your deadline in the booking form.
What payment methods do you accept? +
I accept Lipa na M-PESA (Till/Paybill), Visa & Mastercard, and Bank Transfer. Payment is due upfront before design work begins. All prices are in Kenyan Shillings (KShs).
Do you offer custom packages? +
Yes! If you have specific needs beyond the standard packages, select "Other / Custom" in the booking form. I'll create a personalized quote tailored to your project requirements and budget.
What if I'm not satisfied with the design? +
Your satisfaction is my priority. The 2 revision rounds are designed to get your design exactly right. If after revisions you're still not happy, we can discuss next steps. Most clients love the results!
